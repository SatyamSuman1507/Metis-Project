What's new this month

As the monsoon gently retreats, September in Gujarat unveils a refreshed landscape and a quieter, more intimate travel experience. From the sacred shores of Dwarka and Somnath to the lush forests of Gir National Park, this is the ideal month to explore Gujarat’s spiritual depth and natural beauty without the crowds.

Wander through the vibrant bazaars of Ahmedabad, take in the tribal charm of Saputara, or witness the surreal salt plains of the Rann of Kutch beginning to stir ahead of the festival season. With cooler temperatures and a post-rain glow, Gujarat in September is a photographer’s dream and a traveler’s delight.

This month, we’re heading to Ahmedabad, Dwarka, Somnath, Gir, and Saputara—a perfect blend of heritage, wildlife, and hill station serenity.

Plan your journey now and let Gujarat's timeless charm welcome you this month.
